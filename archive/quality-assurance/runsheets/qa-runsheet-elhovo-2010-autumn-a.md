### Source Divergences

| Date | Field | DPF Value | Diary Value | CSV Value | Resolution | Note |

| Nov 02 | Walker count | 4 (field) | 5 | 5 | Diary correct | DPF Walkers field omits leader |

### Source Reliability Patterns

- **O1 confirmed:** DPF scans remain authoritative for unit numbers. Diary unit summaries contain transcription errors (Oct 23, Oct 24, Nov 02).
- **New pattern:** DPF "Walkers" field may omit the leader when leader is recorded separately. Diary walker counts include leader and are more reliable for personnel.
- **Diary internal consistency:** Nov 02 diary says "First unit: 61553" but lists 61549 in unit samples, demonstrating that diary summary fields can have errors even when detail fields are correct.

### Implications for Future QA

- Continue to prioritise DPF scans over diary summaries for unit range verification
- Cross-check diary summary fields against diary detail fields (unit samples) when discrepancies arise
- When DPF Walkers field has fewer people than expected, check if leader was recorded separately
